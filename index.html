Academic Overview

Dr. Aziz serves as the Regional Director for South Asia at the International Nitrogen Initiative (INI) and holds the position of Associate Professor of Soil Science at the University of Agriculture, Faisalabad (UAF), Pakistan. He is the lead partner from Pakistan in the global project "South Asia Nitrogen Hub" and is actively involved in the UNEP/GEF funded project “International Nitrogen Management Systems (INMS)”.
